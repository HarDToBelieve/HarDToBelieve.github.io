33// BifurcatedGraphicsContext allows you to duplicate painting between two given GraphicsContexts;
34// for example, for painting into a bitmap-backed and a display-list-backed context simultaneously.
35// Any state that is returned by GraphicsContext methods will be retrieved from the primary context.
36
