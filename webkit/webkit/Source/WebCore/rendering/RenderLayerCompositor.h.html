145// RenderLayerCompositor manages the hierarchy of
146// composited RenderLayers. It determines which RenderLayers
147// become compositing, and creates and maintains a hierarchy of
148// GraphicsLayers based on the RenderLayer painting order.
149//
150// There is one RenderLayerCompositor per RenderView.
